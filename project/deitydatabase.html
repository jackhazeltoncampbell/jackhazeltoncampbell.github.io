Auril, goddess of winter

Alignment: Neutral Evil

Domain: Nature, Tempest

Symbol: Six-pointed snowflake

Overview: Auril, also known as the Frostmaiden, is revered by those who dwell in icy climates or who live in fear of winter’s fury. She is a fickle deity, prone to sudden and extreme mood swings, and her clergy works tirelessly to appease her. Followers of Auril often seek her favor through acts of sacrifice, hoping to avoid her wrathful gaze.

Auril - Forgotten Realms

Azuth, god of wizards
