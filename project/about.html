About the Database

For as long as I've played D&D, I've always loved to play classes that focus on healing and divine powers. In other games, it's pretty simple - you just use holy powers, and that's that. In D&D, there's a huge focus on who your deity is, what they mean to you, how you pray to them and use their power - there's a lot to consider for both gameplay and role-play reasons!

Because of this, I'd have to research the various Faerûn D&D deities to figure out what they're all about. Of course there's always the Player's Handbook with a quick reference for some of the deities, but I wanted a full, comprehensive list of all the deities. Their symbols, alignments, domains, and some info on what they're followers may be like. I also ended up including links to each and ever deity's link on the Forgotten Realms wiki, for if you want more detailed, comprehensive information on the specific deity.

And thus, the D&D Deity Database was born! Hopefully you enjoy this simplistic, easy-to-use website and add more complex lore to your campaigns, or simply make more interesting and complex characters!
