The ultimate database for the gods of Faerûn!

Ever wanted to play a cleric, but didn't want to search through dozens of wiki pages to figure out what gods you could worship are?

Or maybe you've wanted to play a paladin, and have had to flip through pages of lore to figure out which god you wanted them to swear their oath to?

Or perhaps you're a DM or player who wanted to get a better idea of the world lore?

This database aims to solve that exact problem - to take all the deity lore of Faerûn, and condense it to a format that's easy to read and access!

Database Features

Each entry on the database will include the following features:

  • Name of the diety
  • Alignment of the deity
  • Domain of the deity
  • A general synopsis of who the deity is
  • A link to the coresponding page on the Forgotten Realms Wiki
